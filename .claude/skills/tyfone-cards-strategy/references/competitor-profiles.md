# Competitor Profiles — Cards Feature Baselines

Use this file as the starting baseline when populating the Competitor Matrix.
Update entries as new research surfaces.  Each section maps directly to a row
in the matrix or a cell in the Business Banking Cards table.

---

## Alkami

- **Digital issuance**: Yes — real-time virtual card provisioning via partner
  issuance APIs; white-label card-art upload supported.
- **Push provisioning**: Supported for iOS (Apple Pay) and Android (Google Pay)
  via tokenization partner integration.
- **Tokenization**: Delegates to network tokenization (Visa / Mastercard); no
  proprietary token vault.
- **Virtual cards**: Retail + business; single-use and multi-use; spend controls
  (amount, merchant category, geo).
- **Commercial / business cards**: Virtual card programs for AP automation;
  expense-category controls; basic reporting dashboard.
- **Visa DCAS / VTC / MLC**: No public confirmation of DCAS participation as of
  Q4 2024; VTC integration unclear.
- **Key differentiator**: Large CU install base; broad open-banking connector
  library.

## Q2 (now part of Intuit Financial Services)

- **Digital issuance**: Yes — card provisioning through Q2 Digital Banking
  platform; supports instant virtual card.
- **Push provisioning**: Supported; integrated wallet provisioning flow.
- **Tokenization**: Network tokenization via card network partnerships.
- **Virtual cards**: Full retail + business offering; configurable spend rules.
- **Commercial / business cards**: Strong SMB virtual-card story; AP
  automation integrations exist; expense controls and receipt capture.
- **Visa DCAS / VTC / MLC**: Rumored DCAS pilot with select issuers (2024);
  not publicly confirmed production.
- **Key differentiator**: Scale + Intuit ecosystem synergies; deep lending
  integrations.

## Narmi

- **Digital issuance**: Yes — supports instant virtual-card issuance;
  configurable card programs.
- **Push provisioning**: Supported for major wallets.
- **Tokenization**: Network-level tokenization.
- **Virtual cards**: Retail focus; limited business-card documentation
  publicly available.
- **Commercial / business cards**: Nascent; less public detail vs. Alkami /
  Q2.
- **Visa DCAS / VTC / MLC**: No confirmed public participation.
- **Key differentiator**: Modern cloud-native architecture; attractive to
  de novo / digital-first CUs.

## BankJoy

- **Digital issuance**: Basic virtual-card issuance available.
- **Push provisioning**: Supported.
- **Tokenization**: Delegates to network tokenization.
- **Virtual cards**: Retail; business virtual cards limited or not
  publicly documented.
- **Commercial / business cards**: Not a current public differentiator.
- **Visa DCAS / VTC / MLC**: No confirmed participation.
- **Key differentiator**: Ease of deployment for smaller CUs; bundled
  lending + cards.

## Lumin Digital

- **Digital issuance**: Yes — instant virtual card; card-art
  customization.
- **Push provisioning**: Supported (iOS + Android wallets).
- **Tokenization**: Network tokenization via partners.
- **Virtual cards**: Retail + early business; spend controls available.
- **Commercial / business cards**: Emerging; less mature than Alkami /
  Q2 offerings as of late 2024.
- **Visa DCAS / VTC / MLC**: No public confirmation.
- **Key differentiator**: Clean UX-first design; attractive to
  mid-size CUs modernizing.
